Guide for Abhay Sidhu's Final Game



  1. The Objective

    2. Your Objective is to move the ninja to the finish line with two flags while avoiding all the obstacles and move on to the next Level which gets progessively harder than the last level.



  2. Basic Controls

    3. You can move all four directions- Press or hold 'w' or 'Up Arrow Key' to go up, Press or hold 'a' or 'Left Arrow Key' to go left, Press or hold 's' or 'Down Arrow Key' to go down, Press or hold 'd' or 'Right Arrow Key' to go right.

  3. Boundaries
    4. There is a black Border around my game, it doesn't stop you from going through but it can kill you and you won't be ableto see where you're going.

  4. The Obstacles
    5. There are many kinds of obstacles in my game, they'll all be introduced in the later levels.

    This is the first obstacle in my game, its a skull, you must avoid it, if you hit it, the level restarts.

    This obstacle is similar to the first one, it kills you and you must avoid it but it moves, unlike the last one, you must move swiftly to avoid these obstacles.

    This obstacle is different than the last ones even though they look identical, it won't kill you but there's no way to tell the difference between this skull and the one that kills you.
