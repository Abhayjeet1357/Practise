Guide for Abhay Sidhu's Final Game



  1. The Objective

    2. Your Objective is to move the ninja to the finish line with two flags while avoiding all the obstacles and move on to the next Level which gets progessively harder than the last level.



  2. Basic Controls

    3. You can move all four directions- Press or hold 'w' to go up, Press or hold 'a' to go left, Press or hold 's' to go down, Press or hold 'd' to go right.

  3. The Obstacles
    4. There are many kinds of obstacles in my game, they'll all be introduced in the later levels.

    This is the first obstacle in my game, its a skull, you must avoid it, if you hit it, the level restarts.

    This obstacle is similar to the first one, it kills you and you must avoid it bu it moves unlike the last one, but there is a pattern to their movements which can be predicted.

    This obstacle is different than the last two, it'll still kill you if you touch it but you can move this obstacle using your mouse you can only drag and drop this obstacle on another one of its kind. To move it, hover over the obstacle then hold left mouse key and drag it to another similar one and let go.
